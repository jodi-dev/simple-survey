SEG3125 S2021 Student Survey

Critical evaluation of UI designs

This survey was created as an exercise for SEG3125 Summer 2021, where students were asked to apply good UI evaluation principles to analyze an existing UI design. The chosen UI to be analyzed in this survey is the website of Dribbble, a platform for designers to show their work to the community.

This survey contain 5 questions and will take ~10 minutes to complete.
Click to redirect to dribbble.com

1) What is your E-mail address?

So we can contact you to follow up with you regarding your responses.

2) Which school year are you in?

So we have a better sense of our surveyed population.

3) Which best describes how you accessed the UI?

Different devices/browsers may impact your response.

4) Do you like the UI of the linked website?

Go with your gut feelings.

Strongly disagree Disagree Neutral Agree Strongly agree
I like this UI

5) Anything else you'd like to say about the UI?

Feel free to praise or shame, any comment is welcome.
